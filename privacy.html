Privacy, in plain English.

Last updated · June 2026

The short version: your story, your resume, and your inbox are yours. We use them to draft and send the outreach you approve. We never sell your data, and we never send anything without you.

What we collect

  • Profile info you give us: resume, questionnaire answers, your writing voice preferences.
  • Outreach activity: drafts, sends, opens, and replies, so the tracker works.
  • Connected accounts you authorize (Gmail, Calendar), used only to send and schedule for you.

What we never do

  • Sell or rent your personal data. Ever.
  • Send email without your explicit approval (unless you turn on autopilot follow-ups, which you control).
  • Train shared models on your private threads.

Students & minors

Knock is built for students, including high schoolers. For users under 18 we require a parent or school-linked account, and Campus deployments are FERPA-ready.

Your controls

Export or delete everything from Settings at any time. Disconnect Gmail or Calendar with one click. Questions: privacy@knock.app.
